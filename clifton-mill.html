Clifton Mill

One of Ohio's most recognizable historic mills and a destination that combines history, scenery, and small-town charm.

Our Visit

We spent the afternoon exploring Clifton Mill and the beautiful grounds surrounding it.

The sound of the water, the historic mill, and the nearby covered bridge made it easy to understand why this has become one of Ohio's favorite destinations.

It's the kind of place where slowing down isn't difficult— it happens naturally.

A Little History

Clifton Mill is one of the largest remaining water-powered grist mills in the United States and has welcomed visitors for generations.

The mill stands along the Little Miami River where water once powered the equipment used to grind grain for local farmers and surrounding communities.

Today, Clifton Mill continues to celebrate Ohio's milling history while remaining one of the state's most popular historic attractions.
