About GO-CA-TION™

GO-CA-TION was created by Mike and Sherry Parks as the home for their creative adventures in travel, discovery, vintage finds, storytelling, and hands-on projects.

What It Means

A GO-CA-TION is not just a vacation. It is a way of looking at the world with curiosity — whether that means exploring a back road, finding a forgotten treasure, or building something with your own hands.
