Three Amish Shops

One country road led us to three Amish shops we never knew existed.

Our Visit

Sometimes the best discoveries aren't planned.

While exploring southwest Ohio, we stumbled across three Amish shops within a short distance of each other. Each one offered something different and reminded us why it's worth leaving the main highway behind.

Finding places like these is one of our favorite parts of every GO-CA-TION.

A Little Background

Amish communities throughout Ohio are known for skilled craftsmanship, family businesses, baked goods, furniture, quilts, produce, and handcrafted items.

Small shops like these offer visitors a chance to enjoy a slower pace while supporting local families and traditional craftsmanship.
