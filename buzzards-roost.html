Buzzard's Roost Nature Preserve

A quiet nature stop near Chillicothe where we found a hidden pond and enjoyed another peaceful Ohio backroad discovery.

Our Visit

We stopped at Buzzard's Roost Nature Preserve while exploring the Chillicothe area.

The trail conditions kept us from seeing everything that day, but we still found a quiet hidden pond and enjoyed the peaceful setting.

Sometimes a GO-CA-TION doesn't go exactly as planned. That doesn't mean it wasn't worth the stop.

A Little Background

Buzzard's Roost Nature Preserve is part of the natural landscape surrounding Chillicothe, Ohio, with wooded trails, overlooks, and quiet places to explore.

Nature preserves like this give visitors a chance to slow down, step away from busy roads, and experience a quieter side of Ohio.
