Adena Mansion Flag Raising

A patriotic moment during the America 250 Celebration at historic Adena Mansion & Gardens.

Our Visit

We visited Adena Mansion & Gardens during the America 250 Celebration, where living history demonstrations brought Ohio's early years to life.

One of the memorable moments was the ceremonial raising of the American flag. Surrounded by historic buildings, period clothing, and military demonstrations, it created a meaningful connection to the people who helped shape our country.

It was a simple ceremony, but one that fit perfectly with the spirit of the day and made our visit even more memorable.

A Little History

Adena Mansion was the home of Thomas Worthington, one of Ohio's founding leaders and the state's sixth governor.

The mansion and surrounding grounds preserve an important chapter of Ohio's early history while hosting educational programs and living history events throughout the year.

Events like the America 250 Celebration help connect today's visitors with the people, traditions, and stories that shaped our nation.
