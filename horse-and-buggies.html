Horse & Buggies

A quiet reminder that some Ohio roads still move at a different pace.

Our Visit

While exploring Ohio's backroads, we often share the road with horse-drawn buggies traveling between homes, farms, and small communities.

Seeing a buggy around the next bend naturally changes the pace of the drive and reminds us that these roads belong to everyone.

Moments like these are part of what makes traveling through Ohio's Amish country such a unique experience.

A Little Background

Ohio is home to some of the largest Amish communities in the United States.

Horse-drawn transportation remains an important part of everyday life for many Amish families, making respectful driving and patience an important part of visiting these communities.

Sharing the road safely helps preserve the peaceful pace that makes these areas so enjoyable to explore.
