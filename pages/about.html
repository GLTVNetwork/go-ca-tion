About Mike & Sherry

We’re two Ohio creators who love exploring, building, collecting, and telling stories. GO-CA-TION™ is our shared world — a place where travel, vintage charm, and handmade projects come together.

Our Story

We started GO-CA-TION™ because we wanted a home for everything we create — the road trips, the flea market finds, the workshop builds, and the stories that connect them.

Ohio has always been our backdrop. From waterfalls and historic mills to antique barns and small-town strolls, we’ve found that every place has a story worth sharing.

Whether we’re filming an episode, hunting for vintage treasures, or building something in the workshop, our goal is simple: create things that feel warm, real, and full of character.
