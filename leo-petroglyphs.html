Leo Petroglyphs

Ancient carvings hidden in Ohio, preserved as a quiet reminder of the people who were here long before us.

Our Visit

Leo Petroglyphs is one of those places that makes you stop and think about how much history is still around us.

The carvings are protected today, but standing near them still gives you a sense of connection to something much older than the roads, towns, and buildings around us.

It was a quiet stop, and one that reminded us that GO-CA-TION is not always about where we are going — sometimes it is about noticing what has been there all along.

A Little History

Leo Petroglyphs preserves ancient Native American rock carvings cut into sandstone in southern Ohio.

The carvings include figures and symbols that offer a glimpse into the lives, beliefs, and stories of the people who created them.

Today the site helps protect this rare piece of Ohio's ancient history for future generations.
